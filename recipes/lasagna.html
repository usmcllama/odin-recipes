Lasagna

Items you need for this Lasagna

  • 1 lb. italian sausage
  • 1 yellow onion
  • 2 8oz can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 oz tomato paste
  • 1 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 2 cups whole milk ricotta
  • 1 cup shredded italian cheese blend
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 tsp fresh cracked pepper
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella
  • 1 tbsp chopped parsley for garnish

How to cook this spaghetti cake

  1. Add the Italian sausage to a deep skillet and cook over medium heat until browned.

    While the sausage is cooking, dice the onion and then add it to the skillet with the sausage.

    Continue to stir and cook until the onion has softened (about 3 minutes).

  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and Italian seasoning to the skillet and stir to combine.

    Partially cover the skillet with a lid, turn the heat down to medium-low,

    and let the sauce simmer while you prepare the other ingredients. Stir the saue occasionally as it cooks.

  3. Preheat the oven to 350ºF.

    Add the ricotta cheese, Italian cheese blend, egg, and pepper to a bowl, then stir to combine.

    Set the cheese mixture aside.

  4. Fill a large pot with water and add ½ Tbsp salt. Bring the pot of water to a boil.

    Once boiling, add the lasagna noodles and cook just until tender. Then drain.

  5. Spread 1 cup of sauce ove the bottom of a 9x13-inch casserole dish.

    Lay 3 noodles on the sauce then spread 1/3 cheese mixture on noodles.

    Reapeat the layers two more times!

  6. Cover with foil and bake for 40 minutes.

  7. Add garnish if desired
