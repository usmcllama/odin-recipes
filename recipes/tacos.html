Items you need to make Taco Bell at home

  • go local store and buy Taco bell kit

  • 1 lb ground beef

  • 3/4 cup water

  • whatever toppings you want

How to make Taco Bell at home

  1. Brown meat in skillet and drain grease

  2. Stir in seasoning packet and water then bring to boil and simmer on low heat for 10minutes

  3. Scoop meat into taco shell and add cheese
