Flank Steak

Items you need to make Flank Steak at home

  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

  • 1/3 cup soy sauce

  • 1/4 cup red wine vinegar

  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

  • 1 1/2 tbsp Worcestershire sauce

  • 1 tbsp dijon mustard

  • 2 cloves garlic, minced

  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper

  • a nice 1 1/2 lb flank steak

How to make Taco Bell at home

  1. Whisk together oil, soy sauce, vinegar, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, Dijon mustard, garlic, and pepper for marinade

  2. Place steak in 9x13-inch glass baking dish

  3. Pour marinade over flank steak in the baking dish;

    Cover, and refrigerate for 2 to 6 hours, or up to 12 hours if time permits.

  4. Preheat grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate

  5. Grill steak for 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness.

  6. Let rest for 5 minutes before slicing and serving.
